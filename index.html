Edsa Busway and MRT-3 Transit Map with Travel Times

Compare travel times between stations of Edsa Busway and MRT-3 lines. The travel times layer are updated using data from the public transport crowdsouring mobile app SafeTravelPH. The data here are based on daily averages for the month of July 2023.

Get involved! Learn more at SafeTravelPH.org or go to the MetroMapMaker.com link on stamped on the image to edit the map, or visit the Github repository to contribute and improve this whole site.
